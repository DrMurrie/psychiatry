ASRS v1.1 — Adult ADHD Self-Report Scale

Kessler, Adler et al. (2005) • WHO • 18-Item Symptom Checklist

ASRS v1.1 Instructions ▼

Instructions: Rate each item based on how the patient has felt and conducted themselves over the past 6 months. Part A (Questions 1–6) is the screener — 4 or more shaded responses indicate symptoms highly consistent with ADHD. Part B (Questions 7–18) provides additional symptom information.

Subscales: Inattention = Q1–4, 7–11  |  Hyperactivity-Impulsivity = Q5–6, 12–18
